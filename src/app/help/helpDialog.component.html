How to Play:

Each challenge is played in 5 rounds. Each round you will be given a random verse. You will then have to guess the reference for that verse.

You can score from 0-100 based on how close your guess is to the actual reference, 100 points being the best score. The smaller the book of scripture the closer you have to be.
